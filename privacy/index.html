Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 20, 2026. This is a plain-language summary of how we handle your information.

The short version: We collect what we need to connect buyers and sellers safely. We never sell your data. We share it only when you choose to contact a counterparty or when required by law.

1. Information we collect

When you list a car

We collect your name, contact information, vehicle details (VIN, photos, condition description, asking price), and your location for listing purposes.

When you inquire about a car

We collect your name, contact information, and the message you choose to send to the seller.

When you apply to be a dealer

We collect business information including dealership name, your role, business email and phone, DMS provider, and disposition volume.

Automatic collection

Like most websites, we collect basic usage data via Google Analytics: pages visited, approximate location, device type, and referral source. This helps us improve the product.

2. How we use your information

  • To connect buyers and sellers who choose to interact
  • To send transaction-related emails (listing confirmations, inquiry notifications, account updates)
  • To prevent fraud and protect our users
  • To improve the platform based on aggregated usage patterns
  • To comply with legal obligations when required

3. Information we share

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only in these specific cases:

  • With counterparties you choose to contact — if you inquire about a car, the seller sees your name and contact info
  • With service providers — companies that help us operate (hosting, email delivery, analytics). These providers are contractually limited to using your data only on our behalf
  • When legally required — in response to a valid subpoena, court order, or similar legal process

4. Third-party services we use

We use these trusted providers. Each has its own privacy policy.

  • Netlify — website hosting
  • Airtable — database storage
  • Resend — transactional email delivery
  • BunnyCDN — vehicle photo storage
  • Google Analytics — usage analytics
  • MarketCheck & NHTSA — vehicle pricing and VIN decoding
  • AutoFi — financing referrals (only when you choose to apply)

5. Your rights

You can:

  • Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Ask us to delete your data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Opt out of marketing emails at any time (transactional emails will continue)

Send requests to hello@12belowcars.com. We respond within 30 days.

6. Data security

We use industry-standard encryption (HTTPS) for all data in transit and rely on our service providers’ security infrastructure for data at rest. No system is perfectly secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your information.

7. Children

12Below is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from minors. If we learn we have collected data from a minor, we will delete it.

8. Changes to this policy

We will update this page when our practices change. Material changes will be communicated by email to active users.

9. Contact

Questions, requests, or concerns: hello@12belowcars.com

Not legal advice: This privacy policy is provided for transparency about our practices. It is not a substitute for individual legal advice. If you have specific concerns about your data, please reach out.
