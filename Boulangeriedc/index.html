Our story

A Paris team. A Capitol Hill corner.

Co-owners Lounes Khenane and Bouzid Aissaoui run five restaurants in Paris, including the beloved Chez Suzette. Their first U.S. venture brings the same craft to 7th and C Street SE. Pastry chef Slama Abdenour makes everything from scratch — even the passion fruit juice. Boulanger Théo shapes each loaf through a two-day process using ingredients flown in from France.

Made in-house, daily
Every viennoiserie, pastry, and loaf produced on-site each morning using classic French methods and imported ingredients.
Priced for the neighborhood
Most pastries under $5, drip coffee ~$4, espresso drinks up to $7.50. An everyday place, not a special-occasion one.
#4 most bookmarked new spot in DC
Ranked by Beli users in April 2026 — just weeks after opening. Over 2 million bell bookmarks.
A dog-friendly patio worth lingering on

Large, sunny, and designed for a slower, more European pace. Bring your four-legged neighbor.
