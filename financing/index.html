Common questions

Quick answers to what buyers ask most often.

Does getting pre-qualified affect my credit score?
+
No. Pre-qualification uses a soft credit pull, which does not appear on your credit report and does not lower your score. A hard inquiry only happens later, if you choose to formally apply for a specific loan.
Can I get financed with bad credit or no credit?
+
Yes. Our financing partner works with all credit profiles, including no credit and prior bankruptcies. Basic requirements: at least 18 years old, earn at least $10,000/year, and no active bankruptcy proceedings. Your rate reflects your situation, but you have access.
What APR can I expect?
+
APR depends on your credit, loan term, and car. Strong credit (720+) often sees 4-6%. Average credit (660-719) tends to land 7-10%. Below 660, expect 11-18%. Pre-qualify in 2 minutes to see your real number.
How much should I put as a down payment?
+
A common target is 10-20% down. On a 12Below car, 10% down often means a lower monthly payment than a comparable dealer purchase with 20% down — because you’re financing a smaller starting amount. Use the calculator on any listing to compare.
How does the escrow protection work?
+
When you decide to buy, you connect your bank through Plaid. Your funds move into a secure escrow account — not directly to the seller. After you inspect the car and confirm it matches the listing, you release the funds. If something is wrong, you cancel and the money goes back to you.
Is 12Below available outside the DMV?
+
Right now, every car is in DC, Maryland, or Virginia. This keeps inspection easy and removes the risk of buying sight-unseen across state lines. We plan to expand once the DMV marketplace is established.
