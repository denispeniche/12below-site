The Adventure

Explore the Rainforest Falls

Walk through a living jungle where colorful parrots appear overhead, exotic animals come to life, and hidden discoveries await around every corner. Every adventure reveals something new about God's amazing love.

Living jungle environments

Realistic rainforest worlds

From cascading waterfalls to dense tropical canopy, every space is transformed into an immersive, professional-quality environment that feels genuinely alive.

  • ✓ Waterfalls and tropical scenery throughout the building
  • ✓ Jungle pathways and dedicated exploration zones
  • ✓ Dynamic lighting and immersive sound effects
  • ✓ Professional-quality stage environments
They come to life

Animatronic animal encounters

Life-sized animatronic rainforest animals move, react, and surprise. Children meet moving jungle creatures up close in interactive encounters they'll talk about for weeks.

  • ✓ Life-sized animatronic rainforest animals
  • ✓ Interactive animal encounters & moving creatures
  • ✓ Photo opportunities throughout the jungle
  • ✓ Safe, supervised, wonder-filled moments

Discovery stations & checkpoints

Adventure checkpoints and hands-on discovery stations turn every child into an explorer. Interactive storytelling experiences bring Bible adventures to life around every corner.

  • ✓ Interactive storytelling experiences

  • ✓ Hands-on discovery stations

Parent Information

Your child's safety is our highest priority

We've built Rainforest Falls so you can relax and your child can explore. Here's everything you need to know about how we keep every explorer safe and cared for.

🛡️

Safety & Security

Every part of the experience is designed with child safety first.

  • Secured, monitored facility access
  • Trained, attentive adult leaders in every zone
  • Posted emergency & medical procedures
  • Allergy & medical needs accommodated
🎟️

Check-In Process

A smooth, secure arrival every morning.

  • Matching name-tag check-in & check-out
  • Authorized pickup verification
  • Quick, friendly daily drop-off
  • Doors open 15 minutes before start
✅

Volunteer Screening

Every adult volunteer is carefully vetted.

  • Background-checked team members
  • Child-safety trained leaders
  • Clear leader-to-child ratios
  • Designated, visible staff identification
