01 · What We Do

Every kind
of glass.

From a quarter-sized chip in your daily driver to a full windshield replacement on a 2024 SUV with cameras and sensors — it's all in a day's work.

/ 01

Windshield Replacement

Full front windshield swap using OEM or OEE glass. Includes molding, rain sensor transfer, and a lifetime guarantee against leaks and defects.

/ 02

Chip & Crack Repair

Resin injection for stars, bullseyes, and short cracks. Usually saves the windshield entirely — and often takes less than 30 minutes.

/ 03

Side & Rear Windows

Tempered side glass, vent windows, and rear backlites. Defroster lines reconnected. Glass cleaned out and vacuumed before you drive off.

/ 04

ADAS Calibration

Forward-facing camera, lane departure, and collision-warning systems recalibrated after replacement — done in-house, not sent out.

/ 05

Mobile Service

Can't make it in? We come to your home or workplace in Howard County and the surrounding areas. Most replacements done in your driveway.

/ 06

Insurance Direct-Billing

We file directly with most major carriers — Geico, State Farm, USAA, Progressive and more. Most comprehensive policies cover repair at $0.

04 · Who We Are

A small shop with a long memory.

“If I wouldn't put it in my own car, I won't put it in yours.”

Precision Auto Glass is run by Dave — a Howard County technician who got tired of watching big chains rush jobs, swap in cheap aftermarket glass, and disappear when something went wrong.

So he opened his own shop at Dobbin Center. Small bay, small team, same address for years. The kind of place where the person on the phone is the same person setting the glass — and the same person you'll find if you ever need a warranty claim honored years later.

We've quietly become one of the highest-rated auto glass shops in the Baltimore–Washington corridor. Not by advertising — by doing the work right the first time, and letting our customers do the talking.
