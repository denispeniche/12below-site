How is the 12% below market price calculated?
We look up live pricing from dealer listings nationwide using MarketCheck data. We subtract exactly 12% and show you both numbers.
Is it really free? What is the catch?
Yes, completely free for both buyers and sellers. No listing fees, no commission, no hidden charges.
How do you protect buyers and sellers?
We verify every participant before connecting them. VINs are checked for ownership and clean title.
How long does it take to sell?
Most sellers attract buyer interest within 7-14 days.
What cars are accepted?
2010 or newer, under 150,000 miles, clean title, no active lien.
