The Shop

Family-run. Three decades of customers who keep coming back.

Ricauto has been turning wrenches in Kensington since 1996, and changed hands in 2010 under ownership that doubled down on what made it work in the first place: straight talk, fair pricing, and repairs done right the first time.

The shop handles general auto repair for imported and domestic cars and light trucks, with deep expertise in Mercedes, BMW, and other German vehicles — the kind of diagnostic and maintenance work that keeps high-mileage imports running well past where most shops give up on them.
